Most non-sociopaths have a Conscience, but not everyone can literally see them.  That's what MoraLife can give you.  By interacting with your Conscience, you can get a better insight into the type of person you really are.

  • Digital Conscience - Interact with their Conscience.  It will respond to the Choices you make in your day-to-day actions.  You can even customize and train your Conscience.
  • Virtue and Vice - Quickly glance at their most developed Virtue and most troublesome Vice just by opening the app.
  • Conscience Rank - Send your Conscience to Morathology to training in order to gain Ranks and win Accessories for your Conscience.
