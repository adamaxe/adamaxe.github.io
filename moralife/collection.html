By studying Morathology, you can collect Accessories and Figures that will augment your Conscience's abilities. Here, you can review the progress you've made in MoraLife.
  • Accessories - Review the Accessories and features that you've won or bought and apply your Conscience's Moral Armor.
  • Figures - Research some of history's most prominent moral figures from every culture and region.
  • Morals - Track exactly how mature or decadent each of your practiced Virtues and Vices have become.
